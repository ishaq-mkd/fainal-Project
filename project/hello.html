Jamia Hasaniyya

Jamia Hasaniyya Islamiyya, an educational institute
with various institutions imparting religious secular learning,
was established in 1994, in the memory of great
and dynamic religious scholar E.K Hassan Musliyar.
It is situated at Kallekkad, 8 kms away from Palakkad town.
In the eastern zone of Kerala, Muslims mingled with people
of other faiths, are lagging behind the society in
socio-cultural fields. In order to promote their religious
and material education and provide them required helps
in economic and health spheres, Hasaniyya renders its best.
